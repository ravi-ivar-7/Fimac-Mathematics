About

Here, Numerical methods like Numerical-Differentiation, Numerical-Integration, Solutions of Algebraic/Transcendental Equations, are solved with detailed solution and output of these Numerical methods is plotted wherever possibe.

Avoid giving random inputs. This might cause a Server-Outage. A timelimit (usually 20 seconds) is set for each computation to avoid such situation. However in some cases, ( like ERROR: Exceeds the limit for integer string conversion;) timeout usually won't work and this leads to server freeze.

Despite best efforts has taken to ensure correct and accurate output, there may be instances where errors or unexpected outputs could occur in these Numerical-methods due to various factors.
In Such Case, Let me know either by reporting question or via mail.
